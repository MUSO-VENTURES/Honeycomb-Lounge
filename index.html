Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of the date signed below (the “Effective Date”) between Honeycomb Lounge (“Disclosing Party / Company”) and the individual or entity identified below (“Receiving Party / Collaborator”), each a “Party” and together the “Parties,” in connection with the planning, design, production, and execution of the Honeycomb Lounge alternate reality game marketing campaign, including all associated puzzles, narrative content, codenames, node structures, media assets, vendor relationships, and launch strategy (the “ARG Project”).

1

Confidential Information

“Confidential Information” means any information disclosed by either Party relating to the ARG Project, whether oral, written, or visual, including but not limited to: puzzle mechanics and solutions, node URLs and tracking structure, passphrases, story and narrative documents, radio and print media drafts, vendor pricing and contracts, launch dates, membership credential designs, cocktail and menu tie-ins, and any unreleased creative, technical, or business materials shared in connection with the ARG Project.

2

Obligations of Receiving Party

  • Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.
  • Use Confidential Information solely to perform work on, or evaluate participation in, the ARG Project.
  • Not publish, post, stream, or otherwise reveal any puzzle solution, node URL, passphrase, or launch date prior to the Company’s public release.
  • Protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential materials, and no less than reasonable care.
  • Promptly notify the Disclosing Party of any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information.
3

Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully received from a third party without duty of confidentiality.

4

Term

This Agreement is effective as of the Effective Date and remains in effect until the ARG Project’s public launch is complete, and for a period of twenty-four (24) months thereafter, unless earlier terminated by mutual written agreement. Obligations regarding puzzle solutions and unreleased narrative content that remain unresolved at the end of the campaign survive termination until the Company confirms public release.

5

Return of Materials

Upon request or upon conclusion of the Receiving Party’s involvement in the ARG Project, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all documents, files, and materials containing Confidential Information, including copies, and confirm such action in writing if requested.

6

No License

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any license, ownership interest, or other right in the Confidential Information, the ARG Project, or any related intellectual property, except the limited right to use it for the purpose stated in this Agreement.

7

Ownership of Contributions

Unless otherwise agreed in a separate written contract, any creative or technical contributions the Receiving Party makes specifically for the ARG Project (puzzle designs, copy, code, artwork, or similar work product) are owned by the Disclosing Party upon creation, to the extent permitted by law.

8

No Obligation

This Agreement does not obligate either Party to proceed with any transaction, collaboration, or further business relationship. Either Party may end discussions regarding the ARG Project at any time; confidentiality obligations survive such an end.

9

Remedies

The Parties agree that unauthorized disclosure of Confidential Information, particularly premature release of puzzle solutions or launch details, may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages alone may be inadequate. The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity.

10

Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles. Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in California.

11

General

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties regarding confidentiality of the ARG Project and supersedes prior discussions on that subject. It may only be amended in writing signed by both Parties. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full effect.
